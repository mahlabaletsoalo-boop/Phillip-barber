About Me

My journey as a mobile barber

Hi, I'm Phillip Letsoalo

I run my barber business on the move – cutting hair right where my clients are. After working in a salon, I realized rental costs were too high and I saw an opportunity to create something more personal and affordable. I use my bicycle to reach clients at home, offering convenience and top-quality haircuts.

When I'm not cutting hair, I'm behind the camera, capturing memorable moments as a photographer. My love for detail and creativity shines in everything I do.

Why Choose Me

  • Convenience: I come to you, saving you time and travel.
  • Affordable Quality: Great styles without salon prices.
  • Community Driven: Inspired by my mom’s successful food business and passion for people.
